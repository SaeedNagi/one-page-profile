About Me

Code that ships, quality that holds.

I'm a front-end developer and software QA engineer who believes great products are built at the intersection of two disciplines most teams keep separate: writing the interface, and proving it actually works. I spend my days in React and TypeScript, and just as much time in test plans, bug trackers, and DevTools — because shipping fast only matters if what ships is reliable.

My path started in QA, where I learned to read software the way an attacker or a confused user would: methodically, skeptically, and with an eye for the edge case everyone else missed. That instinct now shapes every component I build — accessible by default, tested before it's called "done," and resilient under real-world conditions.
